My Original Purchases

  • Reverse Shattered Backboard Jordan 1's: $160

Selling Both Pairs

After wearing both pairs probably around 10 times each, keeping them in good shape, I sold them to an online sneaker shop, Direct Kicks.

  • Sold as a Package Deal: $575

Reinvesting

Using the $575 that I made from selling the shoes as a package deal to Direct Kicks, I decided to reinvest this mone into an even nicer pair of shoes. I decided to buy a very limited shoe, the Off-White Nike Air Force 1 Collaboration. Nike and designer brand, Off-White, collabed to release a set of ten exclusive colorways of famous Nike Shoes. I chose the Air Force 1 because the resell prices were sort of middle of the pack for the 10 shoes, it looked very good, and was a versatile shoe meaning it could be worn with both pants and shorts. This exclusive shoe did however come with a hefty price tag of $600.
